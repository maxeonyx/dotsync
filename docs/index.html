dotsync

dotsync is an agent-first CLI that manages dotfiles with scope branches: edit in a repo that mirrors your home directory, commit to the right scope, cascade through descendants, then sync to your live system. 

curl -Lo ~/.local/bin/dotsync https://github.com/maxeonyx/dotsync/releases/latest/download/dotsync-x86_64-linux
chmod +x ~/.local/bin/dotsync
